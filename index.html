About Us

Dezy Dental

Dezy Dental is a trusted dental clinic located in Thirumalai Nagar Annexe, Perungudi, Chennai, dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate oral healthcare to patients of all ages. Led by a team of experienced specialists including endodontist Dr. Sriram and Dr. Bhavani, the clinic combines advanced dental technology with a patient-first approach to ensure every visit is comfortable, hygienic, and effective. With a stellar 4.7-star rating backed by nearly 400 happy patients, Dezy Dental has earned its reputation as one of the most reliable dental destinations in Chennai.

Whether you need a routine teeth cleaning, a root canal treatment, dental implants, or aligner therapy for bite correction, Dezy Dental offers a full spectrum of dental services under one roof. The clinic welcomes patients seven days a week from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, making quality dental care accessible and convenient for working professionals and families alike. Patients travel from across India and even internationally to experience the exceptional care that defines Dezy Dental.
